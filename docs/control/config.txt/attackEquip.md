`attackEquip_`[`<equipment_slots>`](../../References.md#equipment_slots){data-preview} [`<equipment_names>`](../../References.md#equipment_names){data-preview}

Equip the specified equipment on the corresponding equipment slot when attacking with your weapon.

!!! Example
    Equip a Clip of Mustle and a Nimble Clip when you attack with your weapon:
    ```
    attackEquip_rightAccessory Clip [Mantis] [1]
    attackEquip_leftAccessory Clip [Zerom] [1]
    ```
